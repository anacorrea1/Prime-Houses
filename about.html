About Us

Prime Houses: Redefining Real Estate Since 2014

In 2014, an Irish family founded Prime Houses in Dublin, aiming to revolutionize real estate. Born from a personal struggle to find the perfect home, the company bridged the gap between dreams and reality. Driven by a mission of transparency, integrity, and improving lives, Prime Houses evolved into a trusted force in real estate. The dedicated team, empowered by a collaborative culture, transformed the company into a supportive hub for homebuyers and renters. The head office, nestled in Portobello, Dublin, symbolizes innovation and client relationships. Maintaining an open doors policy, Prime Houses invites clients to experience the dedication that shapes their collaborative and enjoyable real estate journey. Since its inception, Prime Houses has positively impacted countless lives, leaving a legacy of empowering and fulfilling home experiences. As the company continues shaping the future of real estate, the Irish family's enduring vision creates a lasting impact on those they serve.
